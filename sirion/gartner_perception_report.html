⚠ Why are they diverging?

ChatGPT weighted the product description (vendor-supplied text on Gartner listing) — which names autodraft, conditional questioning, Adobe E-sign → pre-signature features.
Gemini weighted market scores + broader reputation — pulling from Gartner's category data and cross-referencing Sirion's known strength in obligation tracking → post-signature.
Claude got blocked from the direct page (403) and relied on search-indexed snippets + alternatives pages — which are generic praise with no strong lifecycle signal either way.

Root cause: With only 1.5% of reviews visible, each AI's verdict depends on which thin slice it happened to access. The perception is unstable and easily shifted by a single snippet change.

Strategic Actions
What Sirion can actually do about this
Given that 98.5% of Gartner reviews are invisible to AI, where should effort go?
Critical — Immediate

Prioritize G2 + TrustRadius over Gartner for AI perception

Every review on G2/TrustRadius is fully crawlable. Solicit reviews that explicitly mention pre-signature capabilities (drafting, redlining, playbook, negotiation). These platforms are 50-100x more impactful for AI perception than Gartner.

Critical — Immediate

Control the Gartner product description

The vendor-supplied product description is the single most-read text by all 3 AIs. ChatGPT formed its entire verdict from it. Ensure it leads with full-lifecycle language and balances pre-sig and post-sig feature density equally.

High — 30 Days

Influence which reviews appear on page 1

Only the first ~5 reviews are ever crawlable. Gartner sorts by recency by default. Encourage recent reviewers who use pre-signature features (drafting, authoring, playbook) to leave reviews — they'll naturally appear on page 1 and enter the AI-visible window.

High — 30 Days

Monitor the "Alternatives" page echo effect

Sirion's review snippet #2 (consolidation/customization) appears on 4+ competitor alternatives pages. This is the most amplified Sirion text in AI data. If this snippet rotates to a new review, perception shifts. Track monthly.

Medium — 60 Days

Gartner "Likes" snippet optimization

The public "Likes" snippet is one of ChatGPT's key evidence sources. Currently mentions autodraft + e-sign (pre-sig). If this rotates to obligation/compliance language, ChatGPT's verdict flips. Monitor and influence via review solicitation timing.

Medium — 60 Days

Leverage Gemini's Google index advantage

Gemini pulls the most data (5 snippets) because Google owns the index. Ensure Sirion's Gartner page metadata, structured data, and category tags emphasize full-lifecycle positioning. Google's structured data extraction feeds Gemini directly.

Low — Ongoing

Don't over-invest in Gartner for AI perception

Gartner matters for human buyers, analyst relations, and Magic Quadrant positioning. But for AI perception specifically, the 403 block + JS pagination + login gate make it a low-ROI channel. Allocate AI perception budget to open platforms.

Low — Ongoing

Track snippet rotation monthly

The 3-5 visible snippets rotate as new reviews come in. A single new review mentioning "obligation tracking" or "compliance monitoring" could shift Claude/ChatGPT's verdict. Set up monthly crawl monitoring to catch these shifts.
