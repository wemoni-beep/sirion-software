The Perception Gap

Sirion’s objective is to shift from post-signature specialist to full-lifecycle CLM leader.

But AI platforms haven’t caught up. Across 20 queries, 45% of AI responses frame Sirion as post-signature heavy. Only 15% describe it as full lifecycle. Pre-signature capabilities (drafting, authoring, negotiation, collaboration) are nearly invisible — appearing in just 2 of 20 queries.

The agentic AI narrative (20%) is a bridge — but even here, the agents described are Extraction Agent and Obligations Agent, not Drafting Agent or Authoring Agent. Until AI platforms associate Sirion with “create” as strongly as they associate it with “manage,” the perception gap will persist.
